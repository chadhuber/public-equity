Public Equity

We give local immigrant entrepreneurs the ability to receive vital startup capital through a “direct peer-to peer” model.

Benefactors have the ability to lend money directly to an individual of their choice, while an entrepreneur gains access to interest-free capital even without a credit history or down payment.

We aim to leverage local community partners who refer entrepreneurs in need of capital to our platform. Our partners provide trust for our lenders while providing ancillary financial literacy services to entrepreneuers.
